Privacy Policy

SchulFit · Last updated 31 May 2026

🔒 Offline-first · No account · No tracking

SchulFit is a free app that helps families in Germany prepare their children for the Einschulungsuntersuchung (school-entry examination). We designed it to respect your privacy: by default, everything stays on your own device.

What we store, and where

Your data — the child's nickname, learning progress, daily statistics, and app preferences — is saved only in your browser's local storage on this device. It is not sent to us or to any server. We do not have an account system and we never receive a copy of your data.

Google Drive backup (optional)

If you choose to back up to Google Drive, the app signs in with Google using the minimal drive.file permission. This means SchulFit can only create and read the single backup file it makes (schulfit-backup.json) in your own Google Drive — it cannot see any of your other files. The backup contains the same data listed above. You can delete it from your Drive at any time. This feature is entirely optional and off unless you use it.

Notifications

If you turn on the daily streak reminder, it is scheduled locally on your device by your browser. No notification data leaves your device.

Voice practice

Voice exercises use your browser's built-in speech recognition and speech synthesis. Depending on your browser, the audio captured during a voice exercise may be processed by your browser vendor's servers (for example, Chrome may send audio to Google to convert it to text). SchulFit itself does not record, store, or transmit any audio. If you prefer not to use this, simply use the quiz or flashcard modes instead.

No ads, analytics, or tracking

SchulFit contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party tracking. We do not profile users and we do not sell or share any data.

Children's privacy

SchulFit is intended to be used by children together with their parents or guardians. It does not require any personal information — the nickname is optional and is stored only on the device.

Your control over your data

  • Export: download all your data as a JSON file from Settings → Backup.
  • Delete: use "Reset Everything" in Settings, or clear the site data in your browser.
  • Uninstalling the app or clearing your browser storage removes all local data.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the "Last updated" date above will change. Significant changes will be noted in the app.

Contact

Questions or concerns? Reach out via GitHub.
